Intel Timeline

Milestones that shaped technology and sustainability.

1968

Intel Founded

Robert Noyce and Gordon Moore founded Intel Corporation.

1971

First Microprocessor

Intel introduced the world’s first commercial microprocessor.

1978

8086 Processor

Intel launched the x86 architecture that powers modern computing.

1985

386 Processor Era

Intel introduced the 386 processor, revolutionizing PC performance.

1993

Pentium Launch

The Pentium processor became one of Intel’s most iconic innovations.

2006

Energy Efficiency Focus

Intel accelerated efforts toward energy-efficient processors and greener manufacturing.

2020

RISE Strategy

Intel expanded sustainability and environmental commitments worldwide.

2023

99% Renewable Electricity

Intel achieved 99% renewable electricity usage across global operations.

2040

Net-Zero Vision

Intel aims to achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions globally by 2040.
